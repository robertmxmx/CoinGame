Approximately 1 in 200 coins generated will contain silver and 1 in 30 will be minted before 1900.

The ROI not including "Online Selling" for a pound of coins is 1:1.05 but can vary greatly.

You can click on a coin to flip it.

For best results run on a 1920x1080 monitor on a PC in Chrome
